The Word of God Risen:

is a simple bible reader app with special additions like Parables and the Book of Enoch.

Explore the word of God with extra content such as explainer videos from The Bible Project preceeding each scrollable book of the NIV bible in this App and there is a dropdown to select specific books.

Please see the footer to contribute to the Bible Project & like, subscribe and/or contribute here also!

This is a not-for-profit, time intensive passion project, crafted over 5+ years by a developer in his spare time. Any contributions will go towards the advancement of this project and for Kingdom building for the coming of our Messiah..
And to show the hopeless, that a man like me ( or You) even can be saved.
God wants us to know Him, this is eternal life, that we know our God...
